### X (Twitter) - Tips Thread

**Tweet 1:**
Quick thread: 5 mistakes first-time online sellers make (and how to avoid them)

**Tweet 2:**
Mistake 1: Overcomplicating your store.

Your first store doesn't need 47 categories and a custom checkout flow. Start simple. StoreBrix templates are designed to get you selling fast.

**Tweet 3:**
Mistake 2: Ignoring mobile.

70%+ of e-commerce browsing happens on phones. If your store isn't mobile-perfect, you're leaving money on the table.

(Every StoreBrix template is mobile-first.)

**Tweet 4:**
Mistake 3: Hiding your prices.

Customers hate surprises. Be upfront about pricing, shipping, and return policies. Trust = sales.

**Tweet 5:**
Mistake 4: No clear call-to-action.

Every page should answer: "What do I want the visitor to do next?" Make it obvious.

**Tweet 6:**
Mistake 5: Waiting until everything is "perfect."

Launch now. Improve later. Your first version won't be your last — and that's okay.
